Minutes to Midnight

Last edited 7 May 2015

Info

Minutes to Midnight was made over 2 days during VGDC's 2015 Spring game jam. It's a platformer about prince trying to reach the top of a clocktower by climbing rotating clock gears. At the top waits the princess who... uh, is either in deep peril and needs the prince's help or is eating cake by herself and won't save any for the prince if he doesn't make it!

  • Made by: Max Chung, Theodore Won, Dustin Wang, Iryna Sulimova, Steven Nguyen
