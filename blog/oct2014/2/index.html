This song just released today, and I like it very much. I've been following nekobolo (now known as sasanomaly) for some time now and love his body of work. I must admit though, I'm a bigger fan of his older music, and I do prefer his Miku over his own self covers, but nevertheless, a new nekobolo song is a new nekobolo song.
